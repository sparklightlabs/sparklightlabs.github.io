HAPPENING SOON | Salt Lake City, Utah

Wasatch Wander Treasure Hunt

The Wasatch Wander Treasure Hunt is an all out race where teams (or individuals if thats how you roll) race against time and each other to find a "totem" that we've stashed somewhere along the Wasatch Front. We will push you to explore the unknown in Salt Lake City and the beautiful spaces around it. The lucky adventurer that finds the totem wins 500 dollars! Get on your feet, get out and get paid.

What is Adventum?

We create adventure

We create experiences that get people out their houses and into the wild. From treasure hunts to city wide hide and seek competitions, capture the flag and tackle the T-rex.

You win cash money

Getting outside is it's own reward, imagine winning some extra cash while you’re there. Now you can! Compete with other adventurers for cash prizes with each event. The more that participate, the larger the cash prize, so invite your friends!

Everybody has fun

Need some friends? Or maybe just an excuse to get out of the house? We’ve got you covered. Come meet other like-minded adventurers while competing in our events!. The more, the merrier.

Sick of being cooped up? So are we.

We are a group of friends and adventurers that want to share our passion for the great outdoors. Our mission is to inspire people to explore the world around them and cultivate an appreciation for our beautiful planet. We help people escape the daily grind and add adventure into their life.

So, what are you waiting for? Get off of netflix, grab some friends and let’s bring adventure back.
